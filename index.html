gavel

Official Authorization

Announced on August 14, 2025, and effective from October 1, 2025. This is a permanent amendment to China's Entry-Exit Administration Regulations, not a temporary pilot program.

No employer sponsorship required! Engage in education, research, cultural exchange, entrepreneurship, and business activities with unprecedented flexibility.

China's strategic response to tightening Western immigration policies, particularly targeting Indian STEM talent who comprise 70%+ of US H-1B visa holders.

assignment Application Process

1

Determine Your Jurisdiction

Chinese diplomatic missions in India have specific jurisdictions:

  • Embassy (New Delhi): All states except those below
  • Consulate (Mumbai): Maharashtra & Karnataka
  • Consulate (Kolkata): Bihar, Jharkhand, Odisha, Chhattisgarh & West Bengal
2

Complete COVA Online Form

Fill out the China Visa Online Application (COVA) system accurately. Save your application number for appointment and tracking.

open_in_new Access COVA System
3

Visit CVASC

Go to the China Visa Application Service Center (CVASC) in person:

  • Check if appointment is required (policy may change)
  • Submit printed & signed COVA form, passport, photo, and supporting documents
  • Provide biometric data (fingerprints) - exemptions apply for age 14 or 70+
4

Pay Fees & Collect Passport

Pay the visa and service fees. Standard processing takes 4 working days. Express service available for faster processing.
